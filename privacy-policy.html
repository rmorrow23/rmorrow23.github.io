Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 8th, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Morrow Industries LLC (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information across all Morrow Industries products, including the VictoryVault mobile application, the morrow-industries.com website, and any future apps, services, experiences, or platforms we develop (collectively, the “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

A) Account Information

When users create an account, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Username
  • Email address
  • Date of birth (for age verification)
  • Firebase Auth identifiers

B) Identity Verification for Payouts

For applications offering rewards (such as VictoryVault), we may require additional verification to confirm identity and eligibility for payouts, including:

  • Government ID verification
  • Payment details (e.g., PayPal, banking information)
  • Address

C) Automatically Collected Data

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • App activity logs
  • Error/crash logs (Crashlytics)
  • Analytics data (we may use Firebase Analytics or similar tools)

D) User-Generated Content

Some Services allow users to create or submit content such as usernames or profile identifiers. We do not allow uploads of images or text content at this time beyond basic identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate our Services
  • To deliver personalized or non-personalized advertisements via AdMob
  • To prevent fraud and ensure payouts go to verified individuals
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To communicate important updates or account actions

3. Advertising & Cookies

VictoryVault and other Morrow Industries apps use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (AAID)
  • IP address
  • Device type
  • Interactions with ads

Users may opt out of personalized ads through their device settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with:

  • Google Firebase (authentication, storage, analytics)
  • AdMob (advertising)
  • Identity verification partners (for payout services)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Children’s Privacy

All Morrow Industries Services, including VictoryVault, are intended for users aged 18 years or older. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18.

6. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure servers. No transmission is 100% secure, but we work to protect all information stored with us.
