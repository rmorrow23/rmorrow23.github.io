Account & Data Deletion

At Morrow Industries LLC, we believe users should always be in full control of their data. This page explains how you can delete your VictoryVault account (and any related Morrow Industries apps) along with all associated personal information.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request deletion, we permanently erase:

  • Your VictoryVault account
  • Your username and profile information
  • Gameplay stats, rewards, and earned points
  • Any linked authentication tokens (Google Sign-In, email login)
  • Support messages and app feedback
  • Any other personal data stored in our systems

This process is irreversible.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account in two ways:

Option 1 — Delete From Your Account Dashboard

Visit the official Morrow Industries account portal:

Open Account Dashboard

After signing in, select “Delete My Account & Data”.

Option 2 — Request Manual Deletion

If you cannot access your account, email us from the email linked to the account:

dev@morrow-industries.com

Include your username and a request for account deletion. Our team will verify ownership and remove the account within 3–5 business days.

How Long Does Deletion Take?

Data deletion may take up to 72 hours. Some anonymized analytics data may remain, but it cannot be used to identify you.
